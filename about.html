About Us

Our Mission

At FoodSave, we’re on a mission to reduce food waste and promote sustainable living. Every year, millions of tons of food go to waste while many people struggle with food insecurity. Our app bridges the gap by helping individuals, households, and businesses track, manage, and share surplus food efficiently.

How It Works

  • Track Your Food – Log your groceries and receive smart reminders before they expire.
  • Discover Recipes – Use AI-powered suggestions to create meals with what you already have.
  • Share & Donate – Connect with local food banks, shelters, and neighbors to share excess food.
  • Learn & Take Action – Get tips, challenges, and insights on how to reduce waste and save money.

Why It Matters

Food waste is a global issue with environmental, social, and economic consequences. By making small changes in how we buy, store, and share food, we can collectively make a huge impact. FoodSave empowers you to be part of the solution—saving food, money, and the planet.
