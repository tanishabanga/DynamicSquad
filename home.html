Food Waste Awareness

What is Food Waste?

Food waste refers to food that is discarded or uneaten. It occurs at different stages of the food supply chain, including production, processing, retail, and consumption.

Why is Food Waste a Problem?

Food waste contributes to environmental issues, including excessive waste in landfills, unnecessary water and energy usage, and increased greenhouse gas emissions.

How Can We Reduce Food Waste?

  • Plan meals and buy only what is needed.
  • Store food properly to extend its shelf life.
  • Use leftovers creatively in new meals.
  • Donate excess food to local food banks.
